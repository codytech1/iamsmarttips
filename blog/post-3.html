Everything We Know About Google's Upcoming Smart Glasses Ahead of I/O

The return of smart glasses? Why wearable AI could become a major part of Google's future ecosystem.

Codytech May 18, 2026 • 5 min read
Google's AI-powered smart glasses concept.

Google appears to be preparing another major push into wearable technology, and this time the company seems more focused on AI integration than ever before. Reports suggest the upcoming smart glasses could feature real-time translation, contextual assistance, and camera-powered AI tools.

Unlike the original Google Glass, which struggled with privacy concerns and limited practical use cases, the new generation may rely heavily on Gemini AI to create a more natural experience for users.

Why This Matters

The wearable technology market has grown rapidly over the past few years. With companies like Apple, Meta, and Samsung investing heavily into spatial computing and AI devices, Google's return could intensify competition.

“AI wearables could become the next major computing platform after smartphones.”

Analysts believe Google may unveil more details during its upcoming I/O event, where the company traditionally introduces its latest AI and Android innovations.
